Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 1/4 tsp cooking salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 green onion stems

Steps

  1. Choose pot size
  2. Saute garlic in butter
  3. Bring to simmer
  4. Cook for 12 mins
  5. Rest for 10 mins
  6. Toss the rice
  7. Tumble into serving bowl
