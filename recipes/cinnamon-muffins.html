Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup walnuts
  • 2 cups shredded carrot
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded apple
  • 1/2 cup dessicated coconut
  • 3/4 cup raisins

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Toast walnuts
  3. Spray muffin cases
  4. Mix batter
  5. Divide mix into cases
  6. Bake for 25 mins
  7. Cool for 5 mins
