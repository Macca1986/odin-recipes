Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup dessicated coconut
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 4 tbsp golden syrup
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Line baking trays
  3. Mix flour, oats, coconut and sugar
  4. Melt butter with golden syrup
  5. Add baking soda
  6. Pour butter into flour
  7. Roll into balls and place on trays
  8. Bake for 15 mins
  9. Stand on trays for 5 mins
